Hardware breaks even partway through year 2. After that, the $1,146/month that was going to subscriptions stays in the business. The hardware also retains 50–70% resale value after 2–3 years.

Cloud costs assume no overages or price increases. Actual spending is typically higher. Electricity for both machines is roughly $30–50/month.

The ask

$24,266 one-time investment. Replaces $13,752/year in subscriptions that come with usage limits. Both of us get our own private AI with no restrictions, accessible from anywhere. The hardware is a tangible asset you own — it sits on a rack in the office, and new AI models are free downloads that make it more capable over time.
